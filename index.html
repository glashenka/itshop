ITShop aims to deliver a vast selection of computer and electronics related products at competitive pricing accompanied by the best service. ITShop sells branded laptops and tablets, and manufactures custom built performance towers.

To complement this ITShop also stocks and sells all types of accessories, peripherals, cables, components and batteries.

ITShop also offers service and repair work to the local community for both home users and small business on various devices. These include but are not limited to laptops, desktops, tablets and mobiles.
